## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is CryptoGuard?">

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is CryptoGuard free to use?">
    Yes, CryptoGuard is completely free and accessible to all users, with no hidden costs. Premium API access is available for developers who need higher rate limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Which browsers are supported?">
    CryptoGuard is available on all Chromium-based browsers:
    - Google Chrome
    - Brave
    - Microsoft Edge
    - Opera
    
    Firefox support is coming soon.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What blockchains does CryptoGuard support?">

    - **Ethereum** (mainnet and major L2s)
    - **BNB Smart Chain**
    - **Polygon**
    
    Additional chains are added regularly based on user demand.
  </Accordion>

## Security & Protection

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does CryptoGuard protect against phishing attempts?">
    CryptoGuard uses advanced AI algorithms to scan and identify phishing attempts in real-time, effectively blocking malicious sites before they can steal your credentials or drain your wallet. Our multi-layered approach includes:
    
    - URL pattern analysis
    - Domain age verification
    - SSL certificate validation
    - AI-powered content scanning
    - Known phishing database lookups
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can CryptoGuard detect and neutralize malware?">
    Yes, the extension is equipped to detect and neutralize malicious scripts and codes on websites, including those hidden in smart contracts. Our bytecode analysis engine identifies:
    
    - Honeypot contracts
    - Rug pull mechanisms
    - Hidden mint functions
    - Blacklist capabilities
    - Excessive trading fees
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Why is it essential to evaluate the risk score of contracts?">
    Evaluating the risk score helps identify the security level of smart contracts, assisting in avoiding potentially dangerous ones. Our 0-100 risk score aggregates multiple factors including:
    
    - Contract code analysis
    - Ownership structure
    - Liquidity health
    - Historical activity
    - Audit status
    
    Contracts with scores above 70 should be approached with extreme caution.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does CryptoGuard ensure its security is super effective?">
    By incorporating insights from industry-leading security providers, CryptoGuard establishes a comprehensive security framework:
    
    | Provider | Specialty |
    |----------|-----------|
    | CUBE3.AI | Transaction simulation |
    | GoPlusSecurity | Token security |
    | EtherScan | Contract verification |
    | BSCScan | BNB Chain data |
    | Shieldeum | Threat intelligence |
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can CryptoGuard enhance the security of crypto wallets?">
    Yes, it enhances crypto wallet security by:
    
    - **Address verification** before sending funds
    - **Approval monitoring** to track token permissions
    - **Drain protection** with spending limits
    - **Transaction analysis** before signing
    - **Phishing prevention** on wallet connection requests
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features & Functionality

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the AI Transaction Explainer feature work?">
    This feature provides clear, understandable breakdowns of each Web3 transaction, ensuring users have full transparency and understanding of their online activities.
    
    When you initiate a transaction:
    1. CryptoGuard intercepts the request
    2. Simulates the transaction on a forked state
    3. AI generates a natural language explanation
    4. Shows you exactly what will happen before you sign
    
    Example: "You are swapping 0.5 ETH for approximately 1,250 USDC on Uniswap V3."
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What are the security modules within CryptoGuard?">
    CryptoGuard includes three key modules:
    
    **Analysis Module**
    - Real-time page security analysis
    - Contract risk scoring
    - URL threat detection
    
    **My Transactions Module**
    - Transaction history with security annotations
    - Approval tracking
    - Activity monitoring
    
    **Tracker Module**
    - Wallet address monitoring
    - Custom alert configuration
    - Real-time notifications
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What makes CryptoGuard essential for navigating Web3?">
    Given the increasing complexity and frequency of cyberattacks, CryptoGuard's advanced AI-driven security measures offer essential protection. In 2023 alone:
    
    - $1.7B was stolen in Web3 heists
    - $295M lost to phishing attacks
    - Thousands of rug pulls occurred
    
    CryptoGuard has blocked over 2.4M threats and saved users from potential losses.
  </Accordion>

## Privacy & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does CryptoGuard ensure user privacy?">
    At ChainGPT, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. Our CryptoGuard extension:
    
    - **Never accesses** your private keys or seed phrases
    - **Only processes** publicly available blockchain data
    - **Never tracks** your browsing history
    - **Stores preferences** locally on your device
    - **Collects anonymous analytics** only with your consent
    
    See our [Privacy Policy](/security/privacy) for full details.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Does CryptoGuard store my wallet information?">
    No. CryptoGuard never stores, transmits, or accesses your wallet information including:
    
    - Private keys
    - Seed phrases
    - Wallet balances
    - Transaction history
    
    All analysis is performed on public blockchain data only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I get a false positive?">
    If a legitimate site is incorrectly flagged:
    
    1. Click **Report False Positive** on the warning page
    2. Submit the URL for review
    3. Our team reviews within 24 hours
    4. If confirmed, the site is added to our whitelist
    
    You can also add trusted domains to your personal whitelist in settings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Does CryptoGuard slow down my browsing?">

    - Average scan time: <50ms
