## Feature Requests & RFCs

We follow an "RFC first" development process. To propose major features:

1. Fork the `cryptoguard-rfcs` repository
2. Create `0000-my-feature.md`
3. Submit PR for technical review

[Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/chaingpt) to discuss specifications.
