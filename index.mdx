# Web3 Security, Elevated

CryptoGuard is an AI-powered browser extension that protects your digital assets from phishing sites, malicious smart contracts, and fraudulent transactions. Built on ChainGPT's advanced threat detection infrastructure.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Anti-Phishing Protection" icon="shield-halved" href="/features/anti-phishing">
    Real-time URL scanning blocks phishing sites before they can steal your credentials or drain your wallet.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Contract Analysis" icon="file-code" href="/features/malware-detection">
    AI-driven bytecode analysis identifies honeypots, rug pulls, and malicious contract functions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Risk Scoring" icon="chart-line" href="/features/risk-scoring">
    Every contract, wallet, and transaction receives a 0-100 risk score with detailed breakdown.
  </Card>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Extension">
    Add CryptoGuard to Chrome, Brave, Opera, or Edge. No account required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Web3">
    Navigate to any dApp, DEX, or NFT marketplace as usual.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-Time Protection">
    CryptoGuard scans every URL, contract interaction, and transaction in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Informed Decisions">
    Receive risk scores and detailed analysis before confirming any action.
  </Step>
</Steps>
